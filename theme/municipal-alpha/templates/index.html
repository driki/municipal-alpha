How it works →

99% of these documents don't show up in search

Municipal CMS platforms store documents at URLs with no navigation link. A town clerk uploads a check register, it gets a URL, and nothing on the site ever points to it. Google can't index what it can't find. AI agents browse the same pages Google does. In our testing, roughly 99% of the documents we ingest are invisible to search engines and AI assistants. They are public record, sitting on public servers, at URLs that nothing links to.

We find them because we built connectors for each municipal CMS platform, not a general-purpose crawler.
