## See municipal site work and environmental projects before the RFP drops

Municipal capital plans, environmental assessments, and construction
bids signal upcoming geotechnical and testing work months before a
formal RFP is issued. A town that budgets for a new fire station
needs site borings. A dam safety report triggers a remediation RFP.
A PFAS detection in one town spreads testing requirements across
neighboring jurisdictions.

These documents live inside municipal CMS platforms at URLs with no
public navigation link. In our testing, roughly 99% of the documents
we collect are invisible to search engines. We built connectors that
reach them directly, across 15+ CMS platforms, and read them the day
they're published.

## How It Works

Every day, our pipeline reads new documents from municipal portals across 50 states. We classify each document by type, extract entities and financial amounts, and cross-reference against federal data (FCC, FAA, SAM.gov, FERC, EPA). The result is a structured, searchable dataset of municipal activity that updates daily.

For engineering and infrastructure firms, this means early visibility into where municipalities are investing, what they're planning, and when the RFP is coming.
