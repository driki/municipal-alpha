## See municipal infrastructure projects months before the RFP drops

Municipal capital improvement plans, budget line items, and selectboard
discussions signal future engineering work 6-18 months before a formal RFP
is issued. A town that budgets $4M for a wastewater plant upgrade this year
will issue an engineering services RFP next year. A planning board that
discusses dam removal options in March will solicit feasibility studies by fall.

These signals live inside municipal CMS platforms at URLs with no public
navigation link. In our testing, roughly 99% of the documents we collect
are invisible to search engines. We built connectors that reach them directly,
across 15+ CMS platforms, and read them the day they're published.

## How It Works

Every day, our pipeline reads new documents from municipal portals across 50 states. We classify each document by type, extract entities and financial amounts, and cross-reference against federal data (FCC, FAA, SAM.gov, FERC, EPA). The result is a structured, searchable dataset of municipal activity that updates daily.

For engineering and infrastructure firms, this means early visibility into where municipalities are investing, what they're planning, and when the RFP is coming.
