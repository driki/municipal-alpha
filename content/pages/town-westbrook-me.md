## About Westbrook

Westbrook is a gateway city on the Presumpscot River, directly west of Portland in Cumberland County. A former mill town, Westbrook is undergoing significant redevelopment, particularly along the riverfront near Saccarappa Falls and the Rock Row mixed-use entertainment district on the city's western edge. IDEXX Laboratories, the city's largest employer, is headquartered here. With 41 active boards producing public documents, Westbrook generates substantial municipal government activity for a city its size, reflecting the pace of change in greater Portland's most active redevelopment corridor.
