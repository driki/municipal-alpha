## About Sanford

Sanford is the largest city in York County by area, incorporating the village of Springvale. A former textile and shoe manufacturing center, the city is reinventing itself around the Sanford Regional Airport, which has a 6,000-foot runway and an emerging aviation and light industrial district. The Number One Pond area and downtown Sanford are active redevelopment zones. The city operates through a mayor-council government with eight committees covering everything from budget and finance to economic development and community services.
