## About Lewiston

Lewiston sits on the Androscoggin River across from its twin city Auburn. The city's economy has transitioned from textile manufacturing to healthcare, education, and services. The Continental Mill and Bates Mill complexes are being redeveloped into mixed-use spaces. Lewiston has one of Maine's most diverse populations, with a significant Somali and immigrant community that has revitalized downtown neighborhoods. Bates College, a top liberal arts school, anchors the north end. The city operates through an extensive board and committee structure covering planning, economic development, housing, and community services.
