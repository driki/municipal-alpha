## About Saco

Saco sits on the north bank of the Saco River, across from Biddeford. The city mixes a historic mill district with suburban residential neighborhoods and the redeveloping Saco Island. Ferry Beach State Park and the Camp Ellis coastal neighborhood draw seasonal visitors. With proximity to both Portland and southern Maine beaches, Saco has seen steady residential growth. The city's 14 boards and committees cover planning, conservation, historic preservation, and community development.
