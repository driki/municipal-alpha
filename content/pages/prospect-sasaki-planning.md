## See municipal planning and design projects as they emerge

Municipal master plans, zoning amendments, and resilience studies
signal future design work 6-24 months before a formal RFP is issued.
A planning board that discusses a waterfront master plan in March will
solicit design proposals by fall. A town that adopts a climate action
plan will fund resilience projects in the next budget cycle.

These documents live inside municipal CMS platforms at URLs with no
public navigation link. Planning board agendas, zoning hearing notices,
and master plan drafts are uploaded to portals but never linked from
public navigation. We built connectors that reach them directly, across
15+ CMS platforms, and read them the day they're published.

## How It Works

Every day, our pipeline reads new documents from municipal portals across 50 states. We classify each document by type, extract entities and financial amounts, and cross-reference against federal data (FCC, FAA, SAM.gov, FERC, EPA). The result is a structured, searchable dataset of municipal activity that updates daily.

For engineering and infrastructure firms, this means early visibility into where municipalities are investing, what they're planning, and when the RFP is coming.
