## Know who needs heavy equipment before they start shopping

When a municipality approves a mining permit, a quarry variance, or an
excavation operation, the operator will need equipment: loaders, dozers,
drill rigs, excavators, railcars, hauling trucks. That need shows up in
municipal planning board minutes and permit approvals months before the
operator contacts a dealer or leasing company.

We monitor 2,600+ municipalities daily across 50 states, plus federal
solicitations from SAM.gov. Mining permits, quarry approvals, mineral
rights activity, and heavy equipment bids all flow through our pipeline.
For an equipment leasing operation, each approved permit is a potential
customer, identified at the moment they get the green light to operate.

## How It Works

Every day, our pipeline reads new documents from municipal portals across 50 states. We classify each document by type, extract entities and financial amounts, and cross-reference against federal data (FCC, FAA, SAM.gov, FERC, EPA). The result is a structured, searchable dataset of municipal activity that updates daily.

For engineering and infrastructure firms, this means early visibility into where municipalities are investing, what they're planning, and when the RFP is coming.
