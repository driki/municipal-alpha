<h3>Two signal layers</h3>
<p><strong>Check registers and AP data.</strong> Direct vendor payments from municipal governments, entity-resolved to public tickers with amounts, dates, and categories. If a town is paying a company, that's revenue. Bottom-up, from the actual transaction.</p>
<p><strong>Building permits.</strong> 43,000+ structured permit records showing which contractors are winning work and where construction is accelerating. Permits lead earnings by 3-6 months. Combined with payment data, you see both current revenue and the pipeline.</p>
