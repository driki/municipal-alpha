## About Brunswick

Brunswick sits at the gateway to Midcoast Maine, anchored by Bowdoin College and Brunswick Landing, the redeveloped former Naval Air Station. Brunswick Landing now includes TechPlace (a business incubator), Brunswick Executive Airport, mixed-use development, and the Midcoast Regional Redevelopment Authority. The town's vibrant Maine Street downtown features locally owned shops and restaurants. Brunswick operates through an extensive committee structure with 37 boards covering planning, economic development, conservation, marine resources, recreation, and more.
