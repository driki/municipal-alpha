### Planning Board — March 18

- **20-unit subdivision on Route 100** — continued review of Signature Homes' cluster subdivision proposal. Engineering review by Gorrill Palmer (civil engineering firm, private). Traffic study referenced Stantec (ticker: STN) traffic modeling from 2024 regional plan. Public comment period open through April 1.
- **Solar array site plan** — Revision Energy (private) submitted amended site plan for 2.3 MW ground-mounted solar installation on Depot Road parcel. Fire department access and stormwater management conditions under review.

### Town Council — March 17

- **FY2027 budget workshop #2** — Public works budget includes $340K for road paving (contractor TBD), $85K for vehicle replacement. Waste Management (ticker: WM) contract renewal discussed — current 3-year term expires June 2026.
- **PFAS update** — Town manager reported Phase II sampling results from Portland Water District supply. No exceedances detected in Falmouth wells. Council voted to continue quarterly monitoring. Testing performed by Nelson Analytical (private).

### Conservation Commission — March 19
