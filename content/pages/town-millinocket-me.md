## About Millinocket

Millinocket is a town of approximately 4,000 people in Penobscot County, Maine, situated at the gateway to Baxter State Park and Mount Katahdin. A former Great Northern Paper mill town, Millinocket is in the midst of a community reinvention centered on outdoor recreation, broadband expansion, and attracting remote workers to the Katahdin region. The town maintains active community development programs including CDBG-funded housing rehabilitation and an Age Friendly initiative.
