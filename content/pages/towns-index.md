## How It Works

We use automated agents to read every public document published by a municipality's boards and committees -- agendas, minutes, meeting packets, permits, budget reports. The agents run daily. When enough activity accumulates, a weekly digest is generated summarizing what happened in plain, factual language.

These digests are sourced entirely from public records. We don't editorialize, predict, or take sides. If the planning board discussed it, we report it.
