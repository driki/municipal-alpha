## What We Collect

**Website analytics:** We use [GoatCounter](https://www.goatcounter.com/), a privacy-focused analytics tool self-hosted at stats.municipalalpha.com. GoatCounter does not use cookies, does not collect personal information, and does not track individual visitors. We see aggregate page view counts and referrer sources only.

**Contact form submissions:** If you email us or request a data sample, we collect the email address and name you provide. We use this only to respond to your inquiry and deliver requested materials.

## What We Don't Collect

- No cookies
- No tracking pixels
- No third-party analytics (no Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel)
- No advertising networks
- No fingerprinting or cross-site tracking

## Public Records Data

Our data products are derived from publicly available government documents. We do not collect personal information about private citizens beyond what appears in public government records (e.g., property owner names in public tax rolls, business names in accounts payable warrants). All source documents are published by municipal governments for public consumption or obtained through formal FOAA/FOIA records requests.

## How We Use Your Data

- Email addresses: To respond to inquiries, deliver data samples, and communicate about our products. We do not sell or share your contact information.
- Analytics: To understand which pages are useful and improve the site.

## Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained for business communication purposes. Analytics data is retained indefinitely in aggregate form (no individual visitor data is stored).

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted to this page.
