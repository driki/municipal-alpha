Section 8 · Where this fits

A note on the tools already in your stack.

Brand-monitoring platforms (Meltwater, Cision, Sprinklr, and adjacent tools like Brandwatch, Mention, Talkwalker) index press releases, news, broadcast, and social. Municipal meeting minutes, agendas, ordinances, permit logs, and budget documents tend to sit outside what they currently cover.

That is the gap this report sits in. Verizon's press footprint is well-covered.
