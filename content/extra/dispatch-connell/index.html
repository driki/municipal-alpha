1.2  What is moving right now

A timeline you can pull is interesting. A read that catches what is happening this quarter is the point. From the Planning Board minutes dated March 4, 2026:

Ordinance to be introduced by the Township Council at the public meeting on March 3, 2026, to amend the Mixed-Use (MU) Zone of the Connell Park and establish the Mixed Use (MU) Overlay Zone. The Planning Board is directed to make and transmit to the Township Council, within 35 days after referral... a report... concerning these inconsistencies. Berkeley Heights Planning Board minutes, March 4, 2026

So as of this read: the Council introduced the MU Overlay ordinance on March 3, 2026, and the Planning Board took the mandatory 35-day master-plan-consistency referral on March 4, with Township Planner Michael Mistretta as the reviewer. That is the Phase-4 follow-through from the August 2025 presentation, caught at the referral stage, before adoption. The public ask came roughly seven months before the ordinance, and every step of it sat in the open the whole time.
