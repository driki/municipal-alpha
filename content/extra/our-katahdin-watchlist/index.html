What is covered, and what isn't

Read this before the rest
  • This is a curated sample, not an exhaustive sweep. It shows that the watch-list returns real, sourced signal today. The full pilot runs the whole list on a standing cadence.
  • 7 of your 12 watch-towns are already in the corpus (Millinocket, Old Town, Lincoln, Livermore Falls, Bucksport, Milo, Brunswick). The 5 gaps, Jay, Limestone/Loring, East Millinocket, Patten, Medway, are not yet onboarded. They are part of the pilot scope, not a limit.
  • Depth is uneven by design. We have discovered far more documents than we have yet read in full, so body-text search today reaches a fraction of the corpus. Extracting your target towns deeper is exactly what a pilot prioritizes, and recall climbs as it does.
  • Two of your signals live outside municipal minutes. WARN Act layoffs are state filings, and unorganized-territory activity routes through state LUPC. Both are reachable, on a different recipe than the town-record sweep above.
  • Every date is the document's own date. Every link was checked live before this was sent.
